# Country Guides Overview

This section provides detailed information about popular study abroad destinations.

## United States

### Quick Facts
- **Academic Year**: August/September to May/June
- **Popular Fields**: Computer Science, Engineering, Business
- **Required Tests**: TOEFL/IELTS, GRE/GMAT (for graduate programs)
- **Average Tuition**: $20,000 - $50,000 per year
- **Visa Type**: F-1 Student Visa

### Top Universities
1. Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)
2. Stanford University
3. Harvard University
4. California Institute of Technology
5. University of California, Berkeley

## United Kingdom

### Quick Facts
- **Academic Year**: September to June
- **Popular Fields**: Business, Law, Arts
- **Required Tests**: IELTS/TOEFL
- **Average Tuition**: £12,000 - £30,000 per year
- **Visa Type**: Student Visa (Tier 4)

### Top Universities
1. University of Oxford
2. University of Cambridge
3. Imperial College London
4. University College London
5. London School of Economics

## Australia

### Quick Facts
- **Academic Year**: February to November
- **Popular Fields**: Environmental Science, Engineering
- **Required Tests**: IELTS/TOEFL/PTE
- **Average Tuition**: AUD 20,000 - 45,000 per year
- **Visa Type**: Student Visa (Subclass 500)

### Top Universities
1. University of Melbourne
2. Australian National University
3. University of Sydney
4. University of Queensland
5. Monash University
