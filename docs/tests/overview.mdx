# Test Preparation Guide

This guide covers the main standardized tests required for study abroad applications.

## English Language Tests

### TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language)
- **Format**: Internet-based test (iBT)
- **Duration**: About 3 hours
- **Sections**:
  - Reading (54-72 minutes)
  - Listening (41-57 minutes)
  - Speaking (17 minutes)
  - Writing (50 minutes)
- **Score Range**: 0-120
- **Minimum Scores**:
  - Undergraduate: 80-100
  - Graduate: 90-110
  - Business Schools: 100-110

### IELTS (International English Language Testing System)
- **Format**: Available in Academic and General Training
- **Duration**: 2 hours 45 minutes
- **Sections**:
  - Listening (30 minutes)
  - Reading (60 minutes)
  - Writing (60 minutes)
  - Speaking (11-14 minutes)
- **Score Range**: 0-9 bands
- **Minimum Scores**:
  - Undergraduate: 6.0-6.5
  - Graduate: 6.5-7.0
  - Business Schools: 7.0-7.5

## Graduate School Tests

### GRE (Graduate Record Examination)
- **Format**: Computer-based test
- **Duration**: About 3 hours 45 minutes
- **Sections**:
  - Verbal Reasoning (130-170)
  - Quantitative Reasoning (130-170)
  - Analytical Writing (0-6)
- **Preparation Time**: 2-3 months
- **Key Resources**:
  - Official GRE prep materials
  - Manhattan Prep guides
  - Magoosh online platform

### GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test)
- **Format**: Computer-adaptive test
- **Duration**: 3 hours 7 minutes
- **Sections**:
  - Analytical Writing Assessment
  - Integrated Reasoning
  - Quantitative
  - Verbal
- **Score Range**: 200-800
- **Preparation Time**: 3-6 months

## Test Preparation Tips

### Study Strategies
1. Take practice tests regularly
2. Focus on weak areas
3. Use official study materials
4. Join study groups
5. Consider online courses

### Time Management
- Create a study schedule
- Allocate more time to difficult sections
- Practice with timed conditions
- Take regular breaks

### Test Day Tips
- Get enough sleep
- Arrive early
- Bring required documents
- Stay calm and focused
- Read instructions carefully
